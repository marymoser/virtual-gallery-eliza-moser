About

Classical artist Eliza Jane Moser held her second solo art exhibition on Sunday, March 12, 2017, in Robyn Newhouse Hall at the Community Music School of Springfield.

This Virtual Gallery was designed to recreate the experience.

The Artist

Eliza Jane Moser

Eliza Jane Moser is a local Springfield artist who received classical art training at the Florence Academy of Art in Italy.

The Venue

Robyn Newhouse Hall

Designed in art deco style, Robyn Newhouse Hall is the beautiful performance hall of the Community Music School of Springfield.

The Exhibition

Sunday, March 12, 2017

The show featured both academic and personal artwork completed by Eliza during her studies at the Florence Academy of Art.
